# Objection Handling Library

## OBJ-001: Tally is the industry standard. Our accountants and tax consultants only know Tally.
*   **Root Concern:** Fear of training overhead, operational friction, and resistance from staff.
*   **Recommended Response:** HostBooks is designed with a familiar accounting ledger layout and offers free, high-speed onboarding for accounting teams. In addition, it automates modern features like direct e-invoicing and auto-reconciliation that Tally cannot do natively.
*   **Supporting Proof Point:** Over 10,000 accountants migrated from Tally to HostBooks, reporting a 70% decrease in manual data entry.

## OBJ-002: Tally doesn't require an active internet connection to function.
*   **Root Concern:** Internet downtime could block billing or data entries.
*   **Recommended Response:** HostBooks includes an offline-sync capability for retail billing, allowing invoicing to continue even during outages. Meanwhile, the cloud database ensures your data is automatically backed up once connectivity returns, preventing local drive crash losses.
*   **Supporting Proof Point:** Customers with low internet connectivity in Tier-3 cities process retail billing uninterrupted with automatic end-of-day cloud backup.

## OBJ-003: We already paid for Tally multi-user licenses. HostBooks is a recurring subscription cost.
*   **Root Concern:** Perceived cost increase over software already owned.
*   **Recommended Response:** While Tally licenses seem like a one-time cost, you pay yearly for Tally Software Services (TSS), remote access tools, external tax filing software, and local server maintenance. HostBooks consolidates server costs, remote access, security backups, and tax filing software into one price.
*   **Supporting Proof Point:** On average, mid-sized firms save 40% on total IT infrastructure costs after switching to HostBooks.

## OBJ-004: We are worried about data safety on a cloud platform.
*   **Root Concern:** Fear of data breaches and leaks.
*   **Recommended Response:** HostBooks uses bank-grade 256-bit encryption, ISO 27001 data centers, and multi-factor authentication. Your data is safer here than on a local desktop computer vulnerable to hard disk failure or ransomware.
*   **Supporting Proof Point:** HostBooks maintains 99.999% uptime and compliance with international security audits.
